About Us

CharityBox is a non-profit organization that makes donating easy! Our step-by-step process makes donating as easy as 1 2 3. As you traverse our step-by-step process you will be able to select the type categories (i.e. clothing, food, and hygiene) you would like to donate, select the organization you would like to donate to, and the specific item(s) in which you are donating. CharityBox has many useful tools to help donators, for example donators are able to go to our organization summary page where they can read what each organization is trying to accomplish, also when a donator decides to donate they will be sent an email verification recipt of there recent donation. As for organization that wish to join CharityBox we have a sign-up page that allows new organizations to join.

If you have any problems you can check our FAQ page or contact us at any of our social media accounts (located in the footer) as well as emailing us! We thank you for donating at CharityBox your donations will prove to help many people in need.
